Know About Me

I am a dedicated student with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity, backed by hands-on experience across various platforms and organizations. As a member of the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at Karunya, I’ve been actively involved in collaborative projects that focus on building practical, AI-driven solutions. In my role as the Value Orientation Co-Ordinator at AIMS (Association of Intelligent Machine Learning Solutions), I have contributed to promoting responsible and meaningful applications of machine learning in real-world scenarios. My time at the IIT Madras Research Park helped me explore emerging technologies while sharpening my project coordination and technical skills. As a trumpet player in the National Cadet Corps (NCC) Brass Band, I’ve had the honor of participating in national-level events such as Republic Day and Independence Day parades, representing both musical discipline and team spirit. I have also served as a Cybersecurity Intern at Cisco, where I gained exposure to key concepts such as network security, firewalls, and VPNs. In addition, my work as a Citizen Scientist with NASA involved asteroid research, including the visual classification of astronomical data, reflecting my keen eye for detail and love for exploration. My growing commitment to academic research and scientific writing is highlighted by my recent publications; I have a paper in Metrological and Metrical Science, as well as a newly published work titled "Demystifying Pulmonary Diagnostics: A Novel Explainable AI Framework for Transparent Clinical Decision Support" in the Journal of Carcinogenesis, both of which are indexed on Google Scholar. I also participated in the ICT Academic Regional Final, further strengthening my technical and analytical abilities. As part of the Outreach Club at Karunya, I had the opportunity to teach underprivileged students about ChatGPT and the basics of AI—an experience that reminded me of the importance of knowledge-sharing and inclusivity in tech. With every step, I aim to keep learning, growing, and contributing meaningfully to both the tech world and the community around me.
